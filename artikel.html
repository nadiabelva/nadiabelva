China Warns US will 'pay the price' if pelosi visits Taiwan

Beijing, China Tue, August 2,2022

china warned tuesday that the united states will "pay the price" if US House Speaker nancy pelosi visits taiwan, as tensions between Beijing and Washington soar. "the US side will bear the responbility and pay the price for unermining China's sovereign security interests," foreign ministry spokeswoman Hua Chunying said at a reguler press briefing, as quoted by AFP. Meanwhile, a US ai force jet that flew pelosi to malaysia has left the capital Kuala Lumpul, according to flight-tracking website flightradar24 and reuters witnesses.
